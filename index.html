High School
ENTREPRENEURIAL LEADERS

The ‘Young Turks Business Challenge 2020’ is an innovative business plan contest that aims to encourage young learners to think like an entrepreneur, develop knowledge and experience of enterprise, and apply their skills in a practical way from an early age.

The program enables real-life learning through demonstration of native skills and acquisition of new knowledge in a supportive environment. We aim to create an entrepreneurial support system for students from different academic backgrounds, knowledge, culture, and region who are pursuing high school education.

READ MORE

Getting into the list of the 'First 100 Young Turk' will give a student the head start as one of World’s top 100 high school entrepreneurs.

Who
can
participate?

  • Do you have a business idea that is profitable and, at the same time, brings a positive social or environmental benefit?
  • Are you a social entrepreneur at-large, who is looking to run your next great business idea?
If you have answered 'yes' to any of the questions, then this competition is for you!

Eligibility Criteria

  • 01 Students from classes 9–12 can participate in this competition

  • 02 Minimum number of team members is 2, and the maximum limit is 5

How ?

The competition would have three phases:

Business Plan Submission, Business Pitch, and Final Result.
